Focus on your students

Whether you're a homeschooler, a high school art teacher, or a college instructor, you probably don't have a lot of time on your hands. And like most educators, you'd likely rather spend less energy creating art lessons from scratch, and more energy teaching, guiding, and supporting your students.

That's where we come in. Co-founded in 2001 by Brenda Hoddinott, author of Drawing for Dummies, Drawspace has teamed up with celebrated art educators from around the world. The result? More than 500 art lessons designed for learners of all ages and experience levels, and formatted with classroom environments in mind. Used in thousands of classrooms for almost two decades, Drawspace lessons make a trusted and convenient addition to every curriculum.
